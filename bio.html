About Alice

Alice is a licensed physical therapist with nearly 20 years of experience helping patients recover from injury and return to the activities they love. She graduated from the University of Southern California, one of the top physical therapy programs in the country.

Over the course of her career, Alice founded and led a successful chain of physical therapy clinics, building a reputation for high-quality care and patient-centered treatment.

She has a particular passion for working with tennis players, combining her clinical expertise with a deep understanding of the physical demands of the sport. Her approach focuses not just on recovery, but on improving movement, preventing future injury, and helping athletes perform at their best.

The best way to reach Alice is by email at alice@tennispt.com.

Alice offers convenient, specialized care for players of all levels looking to stay healthy and competitive on the court.
